About foobox

[Info]

Name: foobox
Developer: dreamawake
Blog: https://www.cnblogs.com/foobox
Github: https://github.com/dream7180/foobox-en

foobox is an CUI integration for foobar2000. Providing friendly and modern UI and extensive configuration for foobar2000. Able to adapt to high resolution display (please refer to foobox-help.chm), be good at library management, support multi-types of covers, providing some functions on internet searching and download (music, cover and lyric).

[Development and thanks]

Except foobar2000 and all required components, the following codes are modified and developed to customize foobox：
- Playlist view, playlists manager, cover browser panels by br3tt.
- WSH Cover panel by Jensen.
- Search box by Asion.
- Libtree by Wilb.

[Note]

foobox is only an UI configuration for foobar2000, no business purpose. And please do not use it for business purpose.
Sources of music online search and download, cover download and lyric download are from Chinese websites. Please don't complain about that. If you have API/Script for other sources, you can share with me.
